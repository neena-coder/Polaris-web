About Us

Polaris

Pig Farm

Our pig farm is dedicated to raising healthy and happy pigs using sustainable and humane practices. We breed and raise a variety of pig species, including pure-line large white and pure-line land race. We offer high-quality pork products to our customers.

Fish Section

Our fish section is a state-of-the-art aquaculture facility where we raise a variety of fish including catfish. We use advanced water management systems and sustainable feeding practices to ensure the health and well-being of our fish.

Fish Feed Production Unit

Our fish feed production unit manufactures high-quality feed for our fish and other aquatic animals. We use a combination of natural ingredients and advanced manufacturing techniques to produce feed that is nutritious and sustainable.

Goat Section

Our goat section is home to a diverse herd of goats, including dairy and meat breeds. We offer high-quality goat milk and dairy products, as well as goat meat and other products.

Sheep Section

Our sheep section is dedicated to raising healthy and productive sheep using sustainable and humane practices. We offer high-quality lamb meat products.

Grasscutter Section

Our grasscutter section is focused on breeding and raising high-quality grasscutters for meat and other products. We use sustainable and humane practices to ensure the health and well-being of our animals.

Poultry Section

Our poultry section is home to a variety of bird species, including chickens, turkeys, and ducks. We offer high-quality eggs, meat, and other poultry products, as well as breeding and hatchery services.

Snail Section

Our snail section is a pioneering venture in snail farming, dedicated to breeding and raising high-quality snails for both domestic and international markets. We use state-of-the-art facilities and sustainable practices to ensure the health and well-being of our snails, and offer a range of products including fresh snails an snail meat. Our snail farm is designed to meet the highest standards of hygiene and food safety, and we are committed to providing our customers with the freshest and highest-quality snails possible.
